May Fitness Tournament

A round-robin competition where teams compete to earn the most stars

In teams of 2 to 4, participants will compete over four weeks in a round robin tournament to earn stars. Members of the teams with the most stars at the end of the month will be eligible to win prizes! Each week, there will be a different ways to earn points and a new matchup of teams. Whichever team in a pairing earns the most points per participant during the week gets 3 stars, while the losing team gets 1 star. You can see the current standings on the Leaderboard and you can find details regarding the point breakdown of the week under Weekly Descriptions.

To participate, fill out this Entry Form. Don’t have a team in mind? That’s okay! We can put you into a team and you’ll get the chance to meet some new people.

Each week you will input your progress on a spreadsheet used to keep track of your specific match-up. You don’t need to calculate the points you earn yourself, just put your information into your group’s sheet. Your progress is combined with that of your teammates and transformed into points. See this Exemplar for a better picture.You can access your team’s spreadsheet by clicking your team name in the following chart. New spreadsheets for each week will be posted Monday mornings by 9, so be sure to use the correct spreadsheet! (Feel free to view spreadsheets of other teams, however you will not be able to make edits to any spreadsheet other than your own.)
